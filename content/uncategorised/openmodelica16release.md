<p class="MsoBodyText"><span lang="EN-US">This <a href="http://build.openmodelica.org/omc/builds/windows/releases/1.6.0/">OpenModelica 1.6 release</a> primarily contains model flattening, simulation, and performance improvements regarding Modelica Standard Library 3.1 support, but also has an interesting new tool – the OMEdit graphic connection editor, a new educational material called DrControl, and an improved ModelicaML UML/Modelica profile with better support for cyber-physical modeling and requirement handling </span><span lang="EN-US">(see also <span class="link-external"><a href="http://www.openmodelica.org/">www.openmodelica.org</a>, and the ITEA2 OPENPROD project <a href="http://www.openprod.org/">www.openprod.org</a></span>). The multibody models are now supported.<br /></span></p>
<p class="MsoBodyText"><span lang="EN-US">This release includes the following improvements compared to the 1.5 release:</span></p>
<p class="MsoBodyText" style="margin-left: 36pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Symbol;" lang="EN-US"><span>·<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"> </span></span></span><span lang="EN-US">A new improved open source graphic model connection editor called OMEdit, supporting 3.1 graphical annotations, which makes it possible to move models back and forth to other Modelica tools without problems. The editor has been implemented by students at Linköping University and is based on the C++ Qt library.</span></p>

<p class="BulletItemFirst" style="margin: 1pt 0cm 0.0001pt 35.7pt; text-indent: -17.85pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Symbol;" lang="EN-US"><span>·<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"> </span></span></span><span lang="EN-US">Flattening of the whole Modelica Standard Library 3.1 (MSL 3.1), except Media and Fluid.</span></p>
<p class="BulletItem" style="margin-left: 36pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Symbol;" lang="EN-US"><span>·<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"> </span></span></span><span lang="EN-US">Improved flattening speed of a factor of 5-20 compared to OpenModelica 1.5 for a number of models, especially in the MultiBody library.</span></p>
<p class="BulletItem" style="margin-left: 36pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Symbol;" lang="EN-US"><span>·<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"> </span></span></span><span lang="EN-US">Reduced memory consumption by the OpenModelica compiler frontend, for certain large models a reduction of a factor 50.</span></p>
<p class="BulletItem" style="margin-left: 36pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Symbol;" lang="EN-US"><span>·<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"> </span></span></span><span lang="EN-US">Reorganized, more modular OpenModelica compiler backend, can now handle approximately 30 000 equations, compared to previously a maximum of 10 000 equations.</span></p>
<p class="BulletItem" style="margin-left: 36pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Symbol;" lang="EN-US"><span>·<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"> </span></span></span><span lang="EN-US">Better error messages from the compiler, especially regarding functions.</span></p>
<p class="BulletItem" style="margin-left: 36pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Symbol;" lang="EN-US"><span>·<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"> </span></span></span><span lang="EN-US">Improved simulation coverage of MSL 3.1 except Media and Fluid. Many models that did not simulate before are now simulating, currently 98 out of 124 example models in MSL3.1.</span></p>
<p class="BulletItem" style="margin-left: 36pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Symbol;" lang="EN-US"><span>·<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"> </span></span></span><span lang="EN-US">Enhanced OpenModelica Eclipse plug-in MDT – Modelica Development Tooling 0.7.7 with small improvements, more settings, improved error detection in console, etc.</span></p>
<p class="BulletItem" style="margin-left: 36pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Symbol;" lang="EN-US"><span>·<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"> </span></span></span><span lang="EN-US">Progress in supporting the Media library, but simulation is not yet possible.</span></p>
<p class="BulletItem" style="margin-left: 36pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Symbol;" lang="EN-US"><span>·<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"> </span></span></span><span lang="EN-US">Improved support for enumerations, both in the frontend and the backend.</span></p>
<p class="BulletItem" style="margin-left: 36pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Symbol;" lang="EN-US"><span>·<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"> </span></span></span><span lang="EN-US">Implementation of stream connectors.</span></p>
<p class="BulletItem" style="margin-left: 36pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Symbol;" lang="EN-US"><span>·<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"> </span></span></span><span lang="EN-US">Support for linearization through symbolic Jacobians.</span></p>
<p class="BulletItem" style="margin-left: 36pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Symbol;" lang="EN-US"><span>·<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"> </span></span></span><span lang="EN-US">Many bug fixes</span><span lang="EN-US">.</span></p>
